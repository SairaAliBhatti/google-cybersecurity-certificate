# Incident Handler's Journal

## Entry 1 | Date: June 24, 2026

**Description:**
This journal entry documents a ransomware attack against a small U.S. 
healthcare clinic. The attack disrupted normal business operations by 
encrypting important files and preventing employees from accessing 
patient records and other critical systems.

**Tools Used:** None

## The 5 W's

**Who:** An organized group of cybercriminals gained access through a 
targeted phishing email campaign.

**What:** Employees received phishing emails with malicious attachments. 
Once downloaded, ransomware encrypted critical files and a ransom note 
demanded payment for the decryption key.

**When:** Tuesday morning at approximately 9:00 a.m.

**Where:** A small primary-care healthcare clinic in the United States.

**Why:** Attackers exploited employees via phishing emails, tricking 
them into downloading malicious attachments for financial gain.

## Additional Notes
This incident highlights the importance of employee cybersecurity 
awareness training and phishing prevention. Key questions include 
whether the clinic maintained secure backups and what incident response 
steps were taken after discovery.
